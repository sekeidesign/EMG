Hailing from a cozy Canadian town, JYRYMY is on a mission to bring a fresh twist to dance music, drawing inspiration from the laid-back countryside life that surrounds him. But don't mistake that tranquility for a chill-only vibe. JYRYMY can switch things up from chill house to high-energy dance tunes. His music is all about big beats, grand pianos, and massive sounds that have already racked up over 20 million streams. Thanks to major labels like Universal Music Group and some independent dance labels, he's been making some noise. And he's not stopping there. JYRYMY's ready to hit the road, leaving tire marks on the way to get you grooving at a party near.
