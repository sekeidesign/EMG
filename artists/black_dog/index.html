Despite facing expected difficulties like every other metal band based out of a small town, Black Dog is ready to hit the ground running offering energetic & heavy live sets, injuring necks & getting people talking. Made up of three of the members of the now abolished band Hybridreams, they were itching for a bolder approach to modern metal. This time around they are fully embracing their metalcore & hardcore roots, all with finesse and technicality. Dead-set on leaving their mark on their local music scene, they didn't let the ever-so-real challenge of finding a vocalist stop them from recording an EP slated for an end-of-Summer release, putting their drummer on vocal-duties and getting to work. Starting in July 2022, the trio will release the singles "Slow Death", followed by "Talk Shit" to lead up to their debut EP "Slow Death Society" with the help of Montreal-based record label Hell for Breakfast for distribution.
